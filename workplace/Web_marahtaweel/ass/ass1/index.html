Welcome to our store

Zeitoun Glow Store is a small online shop that sells traditional Palestinian products to people around the world. We focus on handmade items like olive oil soap, and olive oil moisturizer

About Our Olive Oil

Experience the true taste of Palestine.Our olive oil comes from ancient olive trees that have been growing for generations. Each bottle carries a rich flavor and a deep connection to the land.From harvesting to packaging, everything is done with care to ensure the highest quality.“From our land to your table.”

Frequently Asked Questions

What makes our products authentic?

Every product is accompanied by a certificate of origin and a short story about the artisan or workshop that created it. We personally visit and verify each supplier to ensure quality and authenticity before listing any product on our store.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship to over 80 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and estimated delivery times are calculated at checkout based on your location.
